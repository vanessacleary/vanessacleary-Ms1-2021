About

Yoga X, founded in 2020 when our lives turned to virtual and in person Yoga was no longer an option.

Yoga X rebranded as an online yoga studio designed with our users in mind.

Helping our users continue to practice wherever they are and provide the benefits that come with yoga. The world has now changed so we have to and now are on a mission to virtually teach yoga.

Yoga X is designed for real people in the new world, whether you’re a busy mom in need of a quick morning energy boost, live a busy lifestyle and always on the go or a traveller looking to relax with a yoga nidra before sleep, we have a class for you. Whether you want to take a course to learn or improve your yoga style or you want the option to drop in at a time that suits your schedule, we have you covered.

Specializing in 3 types of Yoga and meditation - work up a sweat with advanced HATHA, VINYASSA, ASHTANGA flow, or brush up on the basics in our accessible beginner classes and workshops. Whatever your level of experience, life stage, and aspirations from prenatal strength to mid-workday calm—we’ll help you create an online yoga practice that’s uniquely your own.

Guided meditation classes held daily and available on demand to help quiet your mind wherever you are in the world day or night
