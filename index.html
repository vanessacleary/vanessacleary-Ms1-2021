About

Balance is a virtual yoga studio that was founded in May 2020 when in person studio Yoga was no longer an option. Our mission is to build a community of Yogi's & provide a platform for you to Practice. Anytime. Anywhere. with a team of expert instructors & a vibrant community of like minded people.

Balance is designed for real people whether you’re a busy mom in need of a quick morning energy boost, live a busy lifestyle and always on the go & need a lunch time pick me up or a traveller looking to relax with a yoga nidra before sleep.
Whatever it may be, we have a class for you to help you find the prefect Balance!

Specializing in 3 types of Yoga & meditation, work up a sweat with advanced HATHA, VINYASSA or a ASHTANGA flow, or brush up on the basics in our accessible beginner classes & workshops. We cater for all levels of expierence whether you want to work on flexibiliy, build strength or need a mid-workday calm we’ll help you create an online yoga programme that’s uniquely your own.

Guided meditation classes held daily & available on demand or for downlaod to help quiet your mind wherever you are in the world, day or night.
