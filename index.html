About

Balance is a virtual yoga studio that was founded in May 2020 when in person studio yoga was no longer an option. Our mission is to build a community of Yogi's and provided a platform for you to practice Anytime. Anywhere. with a team of expert instructors and a viberant community of like minded people.

Balance is designed for real people whether you’re a busy mom in need of a quick morning energy boost, live a busy lifestyle and always on the go and need a lunch time pick me up or a traveller looking to relax with a yoga nidra before sleep.
Whatever it may be, we have a class for you!

Specializing in 3 types of Yoga and meditation, work up a sweat with advanced HATHA or VINYASSA, ASHTANGA flow, or brush up on the basics in our accessible beginner classes and workshops. Whatever your level of experience, life stage, and aspirations from prenatal strength to mid-workday calm we’ll help you create an online yoga practice that’s uniquely your own.
Guided meditation classes held daily and available on demand to help quiet your mind wherever you are in the world day or night
