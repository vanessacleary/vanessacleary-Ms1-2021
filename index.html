About

Yoga X is online yoga designed for real people, whether you’re a busy mom in need of a quick morning energy boost, or a traveler looking to relax with a yoga nidra before sleep. Work up a sweat with an advanced vinyasa flow, or brush up on the basics in our accessible beginner classes. Whatever your level of experience, life stage, and aspirations from prenatal strength to mid-workday calm—we’ll help you create an online yoga practice that’s uniquely your own.
