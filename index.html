About

Yoga X is online yoga designed for real people, whether you’re a busy mom in need of a quick morning energy boost, live a busy lifestyle and always on the go or a traveller looking to relax with a yoga nidra before sleep.

Work up a sweat with advanced HATHA, VINYASSA, ASHTANGA flow, or brush up on the basics in our accessible beginner classes. Whatever your level of experience, life stage, and aspirations from prenatal strength to mid-workday calm—we’ll help you create an online yoga practice that’s uniquely your own.

Guided mediation classes held daily and available on demand to help quite your mind wherever you are in the world
